Description:

Tacos are a delicious and versatile dish that can be enjoyed by people of all ages. With a wide variety of fillings, from juicy meats to fresh vegetables, there is a taco for every taste preference. The soft or crunchy tortilla shells are the perfect vessel for these tasty ingredients, making tacos an easy and convenient food option for those on the go. Whether you're in the mood for a quick snack or a satisfying meal, tacos have got you covered. Not only are tacos delicious, but they are also incredibly customizable. With endless toppings to choose from, you can make each taco unique to your own taste preferences. From spicy salsa to tangy guacamole, there's no shortage of flavor options. Whether you prefer your tacos mild or extra spicy, there's a taco out there for you. So why not treat yourself to a delicious and satisfying serving of tacos tonight? Your taste buds will thank you!

ingredients:

  • Tortilla shells
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 diced tomato
  • 1 diced avocado
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • Sour cream and salsa, for topping (optional)

Steps:

  1. In a large skillet, cook ground beef over medium heat until no longer pink. Drain any excess fat.
  2. Stir in chili powder, paprika, cumin, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Warm the tortilla shells according to package instructions.
  4. Place a spoonful of the seasoned beef onto each tortilla shell.
  5. Top each shell with diced tomatoes, diced avocado, shredded lettuce, and shredded cheese.
  6. Optional: Add a dollop of sour cream and a spoonful of salsa on top for extra flavor.
  7. Serve and enjoy your tacos!
